Welcome to Rutland's own technology enthusiasts group

Meet programmers and developers from Vermont and surrounding communities to socialize and discuss all things technology.

Anyone with an interest in coding is welcome, regardless of experience, background, or language preference.

We're trying to grow a community of builders and learners, so if you want to get involved or just hang out with fellow technology enthusiasts, join our mailing list or follow us on twitter and get in touch.

We will be scheduling and organizing our events on Meetup, so be sure to sign up for our account.

History

Vermont Developers Group was started by Zachary Scott in 2012, in order to reach out to the locals interested in programming an technology. The groups focus will be to engage members of the community in learning and discussing technology and software development.

Projects

Hang tight, we will be releasing more open-source software in the future. For now you can follow us on github..
